Burnt out?

Lack of support and direction or family/socialies pressure to be soemthing we are not can literally fry our brains. Nerve pathways that fire togther will wire together. In order to stop behaviours or substance abuse that is shrinking our lives in a pattern or self destruction can feel like a one way track. IT IS NOT. Millions of people have walked through the pain and withdrawal and reclaimed their lives.

group

You are not the only one

When the feelings in our day to day lives become too much we naturally seek escape. Depending on our personal experiences and connections we opt for many different ways to cope. If we become reliant on these strategies what initally gave us relief will get a grip of our lives and take over. Mental and physical health will begin to suffer and eveuntually nothing else will matter. All our potential evaporates and we start to feel lost and alone.

Who else walked in your shoes

Seeking help from people who do not share out experience may not fill the void. Once you fill out a simple and anonymous form and submit it, you will be shown other stories that relate to your own. The idea being that an alcohol dependent mother of three , who is also bulemic and suffering flash backs from abuse is likely to use different startegies to break her habits than a gambling addict you only feels alive in the casino. Different stories need different healing.



Belief
